Never have to deal with drunk texting again!

TextLock aims to make that embarrassing, irresponsible phenomenon known as drunk texting a thing of the past. By creating and maintaining a list of all the phone numbers you don't want to be texting from the bottom of a whiskey glass at 2AM you can save yourself a world of regret. Simply let us know when you're going to be inebriated; whether it's a party, a night at the bar, or a night of solo introspection, and we'll send the signal to your phone to make those phone numbers inaccessible until the next morning. All you need to do is register, or login if already a member to get started!
